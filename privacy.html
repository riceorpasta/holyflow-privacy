Automatic Data Collection Technologies

By using HolyFlow, you agree to the use of technologies like Software Development Kits (SDKs) and similar tools for analytics and essential app functionality.

We use analytics tools (potentially including SDKs from providers like Google Firebase Analytics) to recognize you and/or your device(s) across uses of HolyFlow. These help provide the best user experience, allow us to recognize you, maintain preferences, keep your account secure, and generally improve functionality. They help ensure Member information is used with the correct account.

We collect details of your use of HolyFlow (including traffic data, approximate IP location data, logs, device type, operating system, the functionality requested, and timing of requests) and the resources you access, use, and create. We use this information to provide a tailored experience and communicate effectively. The information helps determine aggregate usage, track usage, analyze data, and improve functionality. We may combine this information for a better experience and service quality.

We do NOT use these technologies to facilitate interest-based ads for third-party goods or services within HolyFlow.

Attribution providers (if applicable).

If we advertise HolyFlow on third-party sites (e.g., Facebook, Google), we may use SDKs to attribute an app download to a specific advertisement. We do not sell your personal information, nor allow our SDK providers to sell it or use it to solicit third-party goods/services. Device ID, IP address and network access.

When you use HolyFlow, we receive data from your devices and networks, including device identifiers and IP address. We may process IP addresses with public location data related to your ISP/carrier to determine approximate geographic region for aggregate, de-identified usage analysis (e.g., understanding usage by country).
