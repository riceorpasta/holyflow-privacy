User Data Deletion Request for HolyFlow

To request the deletion of your user data associated with your Facebook login for the HolyFlow app, please follow the instructions below.

Send an email to the following address:
skyrevealed@gmail.com

Please use the subject line: "Facebook Data Deletion Request"

In the body of your email, please include the email address associated with the Facebook account you used to log in to HolyFlow. This will help us locate and process your request accurately.

We will process your request and confirm the deletion of your data within 30 days.

Thank you.
